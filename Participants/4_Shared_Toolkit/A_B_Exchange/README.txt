A_B_Exchange - official Workflow A <-> Workflow B communications
================================================================

Create this same folder in your TEAM Google Drive (or Microsoft 365).
Markers and Buddies assess the cross-workflow exchange from that Drive folder.

Required sequence (PDF exports of the Word templates):
  1. Data Estate Brief (DEB) - Workflow B briefs what data exists (catalog only)  [Mon Week 3]
  2. Demand Brief (DMD) - Workflow A states ranked decision needs  [Tue Week 3, after DEB]
  3. Analysis Plan (AP-xx) - Workflow B owns how to answer  [Tue Week 3]
  4. Analysis Requests (R-01, R-02, R-03...) - Workflow A formalises asks
  5. Findings Memos (FM-01, FM-02, FM-03...) - Workflow B returns answers
  - Optional curated charts attached to memos

Templates (in ../Templates/):
  Data_Estate_Brief.docx
  Demand_Brief.docx
  Analysis_Plan.docx
  Analysis_Request.docx
  Findings_Memo.docx

Cross-Workflow Handoff Checklist (gate before submission):
  ../Templates/Cross_Workflow_Handoff_Checklist.docx
  Confirm online (tick + typed name/date) - see Programme Guideline Section 8.
  Export PDF as Team_All_Handoff_vN.pdf to your team Drive (not required inside this folder).

Naming examples:
  TeamShortName_WSB_DEB_v1.pdf
  TeamShortName_WSA_DMD_v1.pdf
  TeamShortName_WSB_AP01_Plan_v1.pdf
  TeamShortName_WSA_R01_Request_v1.pdf
  TeamShortName_WSB_FM01_Findings_v1.pdf

Also update the Request Log tab in GBF_Consulting_Toolkit.xlsx.
Chat / Zalo / Messenger is fine for logistics only - not the assessment record.
Minimum three completed R/FM pairs before final strategic options.
